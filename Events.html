PYTORCH : The Machine Learning Bootcamp

Are you using Machine Learning in your BTech project? Or maybe trying to use ML in your hackathon project? Or maybe want to explore what all the hotshot companies or successful freelance developers use in their AI solutions? Keeping the need of the hour in mind BDACC brings you another feisty workshop - "PyTorch: The Machine Learning Bootcamp" where our General Secretary Anshuman Mishra will tell you about the world's most habituated and dynamic Machine Learning framework (developed by Facebook Research), PyTorch. Come on board with us and Go from Zero to Hero in your journey of learning Artificial Intelligence.
